Privacy Policy

Purpose of this Website

This website is used to present Janine Bubner’s professional profile and to receive inquiries for speaking, moderation, panels, media, book-related requests and collaborations.

Inquiry Form

If you submit an inquiry through the website form, the information you provide may be processed to respond to your request.

Types of Data

  • Name and organization
  • Request type and event details
  • Message content submitted by you

Purpose of Processing

  • Responding to keynote, moderation, panel and media inquiries
  • Processing partnership, sponsorship and book-related requests
  • Maintaining professional communication with potential partners

No Public Email or Phone Number

Direct personal email addresses and phone numbers are intentionally not displayed publicly. Communication is initiated through the website form.

Cookies and Analytics

This version does not include analytics or tracking scripts by default. If analytics, newsletter tools, external form processors or cookies are added later, this policy should be updated accordingly.

Retention

Inquiry data should only be retained for as long as necessary to process the request or maintain relevant business communication.

This privacy policy is a practical draft and should be legally reviewed before publication, especially if additional tools such as analytics, newsletters or form providers are added.
